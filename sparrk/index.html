About Us

P.E.A.R.L. Institute of New York is dedicated to the education, advocacy, research and dissemination of information that contributes to culturally competent social work practice and other human services programs serving the United States, Pacific Rim populations and beyond.

关于我们

美国亚太社会工作研究所是一家拥有美国联邦政府501（3）（C）资质，致力于跨文化交流与实践，集教育、倡导、研究和培训一体的非营利组织。我们的宗旨是致力于开展社会工作的教育、宣传、研究及信息传播服务。融合亚洲与欧美不同文化的差异，提供针对于亚太族群以及其他族群的社工相关专业性服务。

The institute welcomes the talents and support of individuals, organizations and institutions who share our commitment. The institute recruits dedicated professionals and volunteers in multiple disciplines to serve as consultants, trainers and administrative staff. Grants and charitable gifts received are used to fund academic scholarships, fellowships, research projects and programs that enhance the goals of the institute.

研究所欢迎与我们共同承诺的个人，组织和机构的才能和支持。该研究所聘用了多个专业的专职人员和志愿者，担任顾问，培训师和行政人员。收到的赠款和慈善礼物用于资助学术奖学金，研究金，研究项目和计划，以加强研究所的目标。
